    clouds.rb shortcuts

    There is some pretty sugar that PoolParty gives you for free.

    EC2 specific

    •    1  mount_ebs_volume_at "vol-4e48ad27", "/vol"

      You can make sure that your master has the volume vol-4e48ad27 mounted at the location /vol. This way, any and all of your data set in the directory /vol is saved in the Elastic Block Store volume. 

    •    1  set_master_ip_to "75.101.162.232"

      This will tell your master instance to requisition the ip address 75.101.162.232 when it starts up.
