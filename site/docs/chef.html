    Chef and PoolParty

    Lets start off with a basic example: 

       1  chef do
   2    include_recipes "~/.poolparty/chef/cookbooks/*"
   3    recipe "#{File.dirname(__FILE__)}/examples/fairchild_chef.rb"
   4  end

    include_recipes specifies a glob of cookbooks that will be placed into your suitcase at time of deployment. These cookbooks are then made available to your recipes

    recipe specifes a particular recipe that you want to use when configuring your instances. Typically you specify only one recipe in your clouds.rb and this recipe calls as many recipes as needed.
